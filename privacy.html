Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

KonbiniRank is an independent fan guide. We collect minimal data, we don't sell it, and we don't show ads. This policy explains exactly what we collect and why.

Who We Are

KonbiniRank (konbinirank.com) is an independent community-driven ranking guide for Japanese convenience store products. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by FamilyMart, Lawson, 7-Eleven Japan, or any other convenience store brand.

What We Collect

Account information — When you sign in with Google or email, we receive your email address and display name. This is stored securely in our database (Supabase) and used only to identify your votes and wishlist.

Votes — When you rate an item, we store your user ID, the item ID, your score, and the timestamp. This is what powers the community rankings.

Wishlist — Items you save to your wishlist are stored locally on your device. If you're signed in, they sync to our database so you can access them across devices.

Usage data — We use Google Analytics to understand how people use the site. This includes pages visited, time spent, and general location (country level). We do not collect personally identifiable information through analytics.

Item suggestions — If you suggest a product, we store the item name, Japanese name, and chain you submitted. This is reviewed privately by our team only.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not show you targeted advertising
  • We do not track you across other websites
  • We do not collect your precise location
  • We do not store passwords — sign-in is handled by Google or Supabase Auth

How We Use Your Data

  • To display community rankings based on aggregated votes
  • To save your wishlist across devices when signed in
    • li>To prevent duplicate votes per item per user
  • To improve the site based on usage patterns
  • To review item suggestions you submit

Third-Party Services

Supabase — Our database and authentication provider. Your account data and votes are stored on Supabase servers. See their privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.

Google Analytics — We use Google Analytics 4 to understand site usage. Google may use this data in accordance with their own privacy policy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google OAuth — If you sign in with Google, your authentication is handled by Google. We only receive your email and display name.

Klook (Affiliate) — Links to Tokyo experiences on our site are affiliate links powered by Klook. If you click through and make a booking, Klook may set cookies to track the referral. KonbiniRank may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. See Klook's privacy policy at klook.com/privacy.

Ko-fi — Our tip jar is powered by Ko-fi. If you make a tip, Ko-fi handles payment processing. See ko-fi.com/privacy.

Data Retention

We retain your account data and votes for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal information is removed within 30 days. Anonymized vote data (score only, no user ID) may be retained to preserve ranking history.

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies — only what is required for authentication (keeping you signed in) and analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Children's Privacy

KonbiniRank is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the date at the top of this page when we do. Continued use of KonbiniRank after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Independent Guide Disclaimer

KonbiniRank is an independent fan guide and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by FamilyMart Co., Ltd., Lawson, Inc., Seven-Eleven Japan Co., Ltd., or any other convenience store brand or parent company. All product names, brand names, and trademarks mentioned on this site are the property of their respective owners. Rankings reflect community votes only and do not represent the views of any brand.

Questions or Requests

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or to delete your account, contact us at hello@konbinirank.com
